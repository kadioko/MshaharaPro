# MshaharaPro Business Strategy

## Product Positioning

MshaharaPro is a Tanzania-first payroll and compliance SaaS platform for SMEs and accountants.

Positioning statement:

> Payroll compliance made simple for Tanzanian SMEs.

The product should feel simple for business owners, structured for payroll managers, and professional enough for accountants managing many client companies.

## Ideal Customer Profiles

### Accounting Firms

Best early adopters because one firm can onboard many client companies.

Needs:

- Multi-company dashboard
- Faster monthly payroll work
- Report generation
- Audit-ready records
- Standardized client processes

### Growing SMEs

Good fit when payroll is too complex for spreadsheets but the company is not ready for enterprise HR software.

Needs:

- Payroll calculation
- Payslips
- Owner approval
- Compliance readiness
- Employee records

### Payroll Consultants

Independent consultants need a professional tool that improves trust and output quality.

Needs:

- Client separation
- Branded reports/payslips
- Audit trail
- Repeatable workflow

## Value Proposition

For SMEs:

- Less payroll confusion
- Better compliance readiness
- Organized payroll records
- Faster payslip and report generation

For accountants:

- Serve more clients with less manual work
- Monitor missing data across clients
- Standardize payroll operations
- Improve client trust and retention

For employees:

- Easier payslip access
- Clearer payroll profile information
- Privacy-safe self-service

## Revenue Model

Recommended model:

- Monthly SaaS subscriptions
- Pricing by employee count and company count
- Accountant plan with multi-client management
- Paid onboarding for data migration and setup
- Optional premium support

Possible packages:

### Starter

For small SMEs.

- One company
- Basic payroll
- Payslips
- Core reports

### Growth

For growing companies.

- More employees
- Approval workflow
- Document storage
- Compliance dashboard
- Advanced reports

### Accountant

For accountants and consultants.

- Multiple client companies
- Client dashboard
- Missing-data tracking
- Report exports
- Audit logs

### Advisory / Enterprise

For larger customers or firms that need support.

- Assisted migration
- Custom templates
- Priority support
- Training
- Internal controls review

## Go-To-Market Plan

### Phase 1: Accountant Pilots

Recruit a small group of accountants and payroll consultants.

Goals:

- Validate payroll workflow
- Improve report formats
- Confirm pricing
- Collect testimonials
- Identify repeatable onboarding steps

### Phase 2: SME Owner Sales

Target businesses with 10 to 100 employees.

Channels:

- Accountant referrals
- SME business groups
- LinkedIn outreach
- Local finance/admin communities
- Webinars about payroll readiness

### Phase 3: Partnerships

Potential partners:

- Accounting firms
- HR consultants
- SME associations
- Bookkeeping service providers
- Banks or payment providers

## Success Metrics

Product:

- Payroll runs completed per month
- Payslips generated
- Reports exported
- Missing-data reduction
- Approval cycle time

Business:

- Monthly recurring revenue
- Number of active companies
- Number of accountant-managed companies
- Churn rate
- Revenue per accountant account
- Customer acquisition cost

Trust and compliance:

- RLS/security checks passing
- Audit log completeness
- Payroll rule update history
- Report template review status

## Launch Checklist

Before paid launch:

- Confirm statutory report templates with qualified local expert
- Add billing/subscriptions
- Add production monitoring
- Finalize onboarding and invite acceptance
- Prepare support process
- Prepare privacy policy and terms
- Revoke temporary setup tokens
- Verify Vercel production env vars
- Run `npm run verify`
- Run `npm run supabase:verify-rls`

## Business Risks

- Incorrect payroll rules could damage trust
- SME users may need onboarding support
- Accountants may require report formats that match their current process
- Data privacy and security expectations are high
- Payment or filing integrations may depend on external partnerships

## Strategic Focus

The strongest wedge is accountant-led distribution. Build features that make accountants faster and more trusted, because every accountant can bring multiple companies and employees into the platform.
