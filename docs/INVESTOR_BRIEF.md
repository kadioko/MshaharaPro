# MshaharaPro Investor Brief

## One-Line Summary

MshaharaPro is a Tanzania-first payroll and compliance platform that helps SMEs, accountants, and business owners run payroll, generate payslips, prepare statutory reports, and keep audit-ready records.

## Problem

Payroll in Tanzania is operationally painful for small and mid-sized businesses. Employers must manage employee records, monthly payroll calculations, PAYE withholding, NSSF, WCF, SDL applicability, approvals, payslips, reports, and audit trails.

For many SMEs, this work is handled in spreadsheets, email threads, WhatsApp approvals, and manually edited payslip templates. That creates:

- Compliance risk from outdated statutory rules or missed deductions
- Time loss for accountants and payroll managers
- Poor record keeping during audits or management reviews
- Repeated manual work across multiple client companies
- Limited visibility for business owners

## Solution

MshaharaPro centralizes payroll and compliance workflows in one secure SaaS app.

Core value:

- Run monthly payroll for multiple companies
- Calculate gross pay, deductions, employer contributions, and net pay
- Generate payslip PDFs
- Prepare PAYE, NSSF, WCF, SDL, bank, department, and payroll summary reports
- Manage approvals, locks, audit logs, and payroll records
- Configure payroll rules without rewriting code
- Support accountants managing many client companies

The product is designed for the Tanzanian market first, with local statutory concepts and SME workflows built into the experience.

## Target Customers

Primary:

- Accounting firms and payroll consultants managing multiple client companies
- Tanzanian SMEs with 5 to 200 employees
- Business owners who need payroll visibility but do not want payroll complexity

Secondary:

- Internal payroll managers
- HR/admin teams
- Employees who need payslips and profile access

## Why Now

SMEs are digitizing finance operations, accountants are managing more clients remotely, and compliance expectations are rising. Payroll is a recurring monthly workflow with high pain, high trust requirements, and strong retention potential.

Tanzania-first positioning creates an advantage over generic payroll tools that do not reflect local workflows, statutory concepts, and accountant-led client management.

## Business Model

Recommended SaaS model:

- Monthly subscription by company size
- Accountant plan priced by number of client companies
- Add-on fees for document storage, premium reports, white-label accountant portal, and assisted onboarding

Example pricing direction:

- Starter: small SMEs with simple payroll
- Growth: larger SMEs with approvals, reports, and storage
- Accountant: multi-company dashboard and client management
- Enterprise/Advisory: custom support, migration, and compliance review workflows

## Investor Value

Investors get exposure to:

- Recurring SaaS revenue
- A clear compliance-driven pain point
- Accountant-led distribution, where one accountant can bring many client companies
- Expansion opportunities into HR records, leave, loans/advances, tax calendars, payments, benefits, and regional payroll markets
- High switching costs once payroll history, documents, rules, and approvals live in the system

## Competitive Advantage

- Tanzania-first product language and compliance model
- Multi-tenant accountant dashboard as a core differentiator
- Configurable payroll rules engine
- Audit-ready workflow by design
- Supabase-backed RLS and secure tenant separation
- Built for small businesses but credible for accountants

## Current MVP Status

Implemented:

- Next.js SaaS app with Supabase Auth, database, RLS, and Storage-ready architecture
- Multi-tenant schema
- Role model for platform admin, accountant, owner, payroll manager, and employee
- Employee records, payroll runs, rules, reports, payslip PDF generation, audit logs
- Live Supabase seed data and role-based RLS verification
- Employee portal restrictions

Still needed before paid launch:

- Accountant-reviewed statutory report formats
- Production monitoring
- More complete CRUD polish
- Stronger onboarding and invite flows
- Billing and subscription management
- Legal/compliance review

## Key Risks

- Payroll rules and statutory templates must be reviewed by qualified Tanzanian experts
- Trust and data security are essential for adoption
- SME sales may require education and onboarding
- Integration with filing/payment institutions may depend on external access and partnerships

MshaharaPro can become the payroll operating system for Tanzanian SMEs and accounting firms. The strongest wedge is the accountant dashboard: one trusted professional can bring many businesses into a monthly recurring workflow.
